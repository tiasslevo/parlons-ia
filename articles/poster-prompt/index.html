C'est modulaire

Le prompt a 5 zones que vous adaptez a votre affiche. Le moteur (en gris) ne bouge jamais — c'est lui qui fait le gros du travail.

Envie de personnaliser ?

Tu n'as pas besoin de modifier le prompt toi-meme. Donne le template a une IA (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) et demande-lui de l'adapter : "adapte ce prompt pour une soiree jazz dans un bar a cocktails." Elle ajustera les bonnes zones sans toucher au moteur ni au negative prompt. Tu peux aussi lui demander des idees : "propose-moi 3 ambiances differentes pour cette affiche."

        Template modulaire
        
      

      

        

[TYPE] — Type d'affiche (ex: soiree, concert, restaurant, promo produit)
[NOM] — Nom principal (le gros titre)
[SOUS-TITRE] — Tagline ou description courte
[INFOS] — Date, lieu, horaire, prix, contact
[AMBIANCE] — 2-3 mots (ex: "tropical festif", "sombre luxueux")
[COULEURS] — 2-3 couleurs dominantes
[VISUEL] — Element visuel principal
[IMAGE REF] — (optionnel) Description de l'image de reference jointe
[UTILISATION] — (optionnel) Comment montrer le produit/objet sur l'affiche
[TEXTURES] — (optionnel) Textures souhaitees
[DECO] — (optionnel) Elements decoratifs
[CONTRAINTES] — (optionnel) Contraintes specifiques

Create a flat 2D poster design, portrait format 3:4 ratio. This is the poster itself — not a photo of a poster, not a mockup, not a poster in a scene. The output IS the poster, ready to print or publish digitally.

DESIGN DIRECTION — MANDATORY:
You are a world-class graphic designer creating a poster that stops people in their tracks. Apply these principles with full creative freedom:

CONTEXTUAL INTELLIGENCE:
- Before designing, THINK about what the [TYPE] and [AMBIANCE] naturally call for. A jewelry poster wants shimmer, glow, premium materials, skin catching light. A food poster wants steam, texture, appetite appeal, warmth. A party poster wants movement, energy, crowd electricity. A luxury event wants restraint, negative space, gold accents. Let the SUBJECT MATTER guide your creative instincts — don't wait for explicit instructions, interpret the context and deliver what it deserves.
- Every element on the poster — from the main visual to the smallest decorative detail — must belong to the SAME VISUAL WORLD. If the main design is refined and elegant, the secondary elements (info bars, decorative touches, background patterns) must also feel refined — they can be simpler, but never cheap or generic.

TYPOGRAPHY AS ARCHITECTURE:
- The main title [NOM] must DOMINATE the composition. Make it massive — it should fill at least 40% of the poster width.
- Create DRAMATIC SIZE CONTRAST between the title and the secondary text.
- The info text ([INFOS]) must be readable but never compete with the title.
- BREAK TYPOGRAPHIC CONVENTIONS when it serves the impact.

COMPOSITION WITH IMPACT:
- DO NOT center everything. Use dynamic asymmetry.
- Create AT LEAST 3 distinct visual layers.
- Use STRATEGIC WHITESPACE or STRATEGIC DENSITY.
- The eye should follow a clear path: biggest/boldest element first → visual anchor → info details.
- TEXT AND VISUALS MUST COEXIST, NOT COMPETE.

COLOR AS EMOTION:
- Use the [COULEURS] with INTENTIONAL DISTRIBUTION: 60% dominant, 30% support, 10% accent.
- High contrast between text and background is non-negotiable.

THE VISUAL ELEMENT:
- The [VISUEL] must be integrated INTO the design, not just placed on top.
- If photo-realistic, render with ultra-detailed textures.

REFERENCE IMAGE LOCK (only if a reference photo is provided):
- The item shown must appear EXACTLY as-is on the poster. Keep 100% identical.

ANTI-AI-SLOP — CRITICAL:
- NO perfect symmetry. NO centered-everything layouts. NO smooth plastic textures.
- Add PRINT-REALISTIC TEXTURES.
- Restrict yourself. A great poster is defined by what you LEAVE OUT.

OUTPUT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Flat 2D poster, portrait 3:4 ratio
- All text must be perfectly legible and correctly spelled
- Print-ready quality, crisp at high resolution

Negative prompt:
3D render, photograph, realistic scene, poster on a wall, poster in a frame, mockup template, camera angle, depth of field, bokeh, studio lighting on a physical surface, white void background, generic clip art, childish cartoon style, blurry text, illegible type, misspelled words, AI glow, smooth plastic gradients, perfectly symmetrical layout, centered boring composition, stock photo aesthetic, low quality, watermark, border around the poster
        

        

          
Comment ca marche

          
Remplacez uniquement les zones colorees en haut. Le moteur (en gris) reste identique — c'est lui qui contient toutes les regles de design, composition, typographie et anti-AI-slop. Chaque couleur correspond a un aspect : l'identite de l'affiche, la direction artistique, la reference photo (optionnel), et les extras (optionnel).
